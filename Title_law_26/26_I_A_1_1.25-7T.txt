<P>(b) <I>Reasonable public notice</I>â(1) <I>In general.</I> Reasonable public notice means published notice which is reasonably designed to inform individuals who would be eligible to receive mortgage credit certificates of the proposed issuance. <P>(2) <I>Contents of notice.</I> The public notice required by paragraph (a) must include a brief description of the principal residence requirement, 3-year requirement, purchase price requirement, and new mortgage requirement.
